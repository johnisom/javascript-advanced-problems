Keyboard Use (Show)

  • Numbers and the decimal point all work as expected.
  • To multiply, press the x or the * key.
  • To divide, press the / key.
  • To negate the current entry (NEG), use the key-combo Alt + - (minus).
  • To clear the current entry (C), press your backspace or delete key.
  • To clear everything (CE), press Alt with backspace or delete.
  • To see the result (=), press = or your enter key.
